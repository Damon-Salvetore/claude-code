   187â†’## Best Practices
   188â†’
   189â†’1. **Define evals BEFORE coding** - Forces clear thinking about success criteria
   190â†’2. **Run evals frequently** - Catch regressions early
   191â†’3. **Track pass@k over time** - Monitor reliability trends

   194â†’6. **Keep evals fast** - Slow evals don't get run
   195â†’7. **Version evals with code** - Evals are first-class artifacts
